Game Rules

  • 4 players total, 2 per team (Red vs Blue)
  • Roll a die to move your soldier forward
  • Brown tiles = Battle Cards that can help or hurt you
  • First team to break through the enemy wall wins!

Battle Mechanic

If both teams land on a brown Battle Tile:

  • Each player rolls the die
  • Add both rolls for your team
  • Highest total wins the Battle Card
  • Losing team moves back 1 space
